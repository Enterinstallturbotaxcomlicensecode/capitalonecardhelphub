Prefer to Activate by Phone?

How to Activate a Debit Card (360 Checking)

  1. Sign in online or in the Capital One Mobile app. Tip: text GET to 80101 to download the app (U.S.).
  2. Select your 360 Checking account that the debit card is linked to.
  3. Tap/Click Activate Your Card (near the Debit Card Tracker), then follow the steps. You may be asked to set a 4-digit PIN.
